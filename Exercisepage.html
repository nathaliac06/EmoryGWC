Best exercises to bust those calories

1. Swimming

In swimming you are using your arms and legs to push through the water. Most people don’t consider it an exercise, but if you do just half an hour of this exercise, you will have burnt at least a hundred calories.

2. Walking/Running

Walking like swimming is not really considered an exercise, but also it pushes your legs to go farther and faster. Same with running.

3. Yoga

Yoga is mostly done for clearing your mind. But it can be hard to do all the hard poses.

4. Cardio

In cardio you dance like nobody’s business which causes burnt calories.

5. Push Ups

Your muscles get strong when you push your entire body’s weight up and down. It also causes calorie loss with just a few of these.
